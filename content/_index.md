## Systems & Performance

I like software that has to work under real constraints: correctness,
speed, and long-term maintainability all at once. Most of my Rust work
lives here, from small tools to bigger data pipelines.

## 3D & Geospatial

My passion sits in Geoinformatics Software Engineering, which means a lot of maps, point
clouds, AI, and 3D reconstruction. I write about what I learn wiring these
pieces together, including my ongoing dive into Structure from Motion.

## Full Stack Development

I'm care about crafting visually stunning, simple, intuitive UIs and
pairing them with clean, well-built APIs. I enjoy the full loop of
turning an idea into something people can actually use, from pixel
details up through backend architecture.
